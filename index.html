International Conference on Networking and Computing Technologies (iCONNECT 2026)

April 23–24, 2026

Mizoram University, Aizawl, Mizoram, India

Organized by

Department of Computer Engineering

About The University

Mizoram University is a central university established in 2001 by an Act of Parliament and is located in Aizawl, Mizoram, India. The university aims to promote advanced education and research in various disciplines, contributing to the socio-economic development of the North Eastern region. It offers UG, PG, and Ph.D. programs across multiple schools such as science, engineering, humanities, and social sciences. The campus is known for its scenic beauty and eco-friendly environment, providing a peaceful atmosphere for learning. Mizoram University is recognized for its commitment to academic excellence, innovation, and community engagement.

About The Conference

The International Conference on Networking and Computing Technologies (iCONNECT 2026), being held for two days, aims to highlight recent advancements and innovations in networking and computing domains. The major focus of the conference is on presentations and discussions covering the latest developments in Computer Engineering and related fields from both national and international participants. Special emphasis will be given to emerging technologies through insightful keynote sessions by eminent speakers. iCONNECT 2026 serves as a premier platform for researchers, academicians, and industry experts to share innovative ideas, research outcomes, and practical experiences that contribute to the growth of networking and computing technologies.

About The Department

The Department of Computer Engineering was established in the year 2011 under the School of Engineering and Technology (SET), offering a 4-year Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) course in Computer Engineering. All classrooms are equipped with ICT facilities. The department has a dedicated state-of-the-art High-Performance Computing Laboratory with a 1:1 computer-student ratio, guided by teachers and technical staff. The primary focus is to impart quality education in the field of Computer Science & Engineering with practical knowledge. Students gain valuable industrial exposure through the industrial programs offered in the course.
